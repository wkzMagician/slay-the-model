The Silent
70 (66 14) Health
 
A deadly huntress from the foglands. Eradicates foes with daggers and poisons.

The Silent is one of the 4 playable characters in Slay the Spire. She starts with 70 (66 14) max HP, the lowest of all characters.

Unlocks

The Silent is unlocked by completing (does not have to be a win) a run with the Ironclad.

Unlock Number Rewards
1 Bane, Catalyst, and Corpse Explosion
2 Du-Vu Doll, Smiling Mask, and Tiny Chest
3 Cloak and Dagger, Accuracy, and Storm of Steel
4 Art of War, The Courier, and Pandora's Box
5 Concentrate, Setup, and Grand Finale

Lore

When completing the Act 3 event Sensory Stone, you 'recall' what appear to be memories regarding the pasts of the characters. The TRIUMPH dialogue may recall a battle between the Silent and the Nemesis, and her reason for climbing the Spire.

TRIUMPH

The remains of a ghostly creature sink slowly into the mud before you, barely visible in the moonlight. You have proven yourself amongst your sisters.

Standing victoriously, you wait in silence as the others ceremoniously place the creature's skull atop your head. The ritual has concluded.
