The Ironclad
80 (75 14) Health
 
The remaining soldier of the Ironclads. Sold his soul to harness demonic energies.

The Ironclad is one of 4 characters that you can play with. Ironclad is unlocked from the start of the game. He starts with 80 (75 14) max HP, the highest of all characters.

Lore

  • When completing the Act 3 event Sensory Stone, you 'recall' what appear to be memories regarding the pasts of the characters. The FEAR dialogue option may recall a battle between the Ironclad and some demonic creature.

FEAR.

A demonic creature towers above you, wings spread wide as it howls with laughter. Dead bodies of a tribe surround you while the village is engulfed in terrible dark flames.

The demon calls out, taunting you.

"YOU REALLY ARE THE STRONGEST NOW! Haha... HEHE... HAHAHAAAAH!!"

This laughter echoes forever...

  • This demon may be related to the demon depicted in the card art for Demon Form, or the hand depicted in Burning Pact.
  • The Ironclad's name is not Steve.[1]
