Matryoshka

From Slay the Spire Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Matryoshka
Uncommon Relic
Any Class
The next 2 non-boss chests you open contain 2 Relics.
A stackable set of painted dolls. The paint depicts an unknown bird with white eyes and blue feathers.

Matryoshka is an Uncommon Relic. Each extra relic has a 25% chance of being Uncommon and 75% chance of being Common. Only the bottom-most relic in a Chest may be linked to the Sapphire Key.

Sources

  • Can be dropped by fighting Elites, by buying from the Merchant, or found in a Chest. See Chest and non-Chest chance at Relics > Rarity.
  • Can be obtained from the relics Calling Bell, Shovel, and Black Star.
    • Calling Bell's Uncommon relic can be Matryoshka.
  • Can be obtained from the following Events:
    • We Meet Again!: Can give Matryoshka in exchange for either a Potion, some Gold, or a Card.
    • Wheel of Change: Can drop Matryoshka as a prize.
    • Big Fish: Can give Matryoshka along with Regret.
    • Dead Adventurer: Either by choosing [Search] and getting Matryoshka as a reward, or by fighting the Elite (which can drop Matryoshka).
    • Scrap Ooze: Can give Matryoshka with an X% chance, for increasing amounts of damage and likelihood of success on repeated attempts.
    • Pleading Vagrant: Can give Matryoshka in exchange for either 85 Gold by choosing [Offer Gold], or Shame by choosing [Rob].
    • The Colosseum: The [VICTORY] option drops 1 Uncommon relic (which can be Matryoshka), and 1 Rare relic.
    • The Mausoleum: Can give Matryoshka along with Writhe.

Spawn Restrictions

Can only be obtained below floor 41 (2 floors below the mid-Act Treasure room in Act 3).

Interactions

Synergies

  • Tiny Chest can allow the extra relics provided by Matryoshka to be obtained sooner than normal thanks to its extra Treasure Room generation.

Neutral

  • Having both Matryoshka and N'loth's Hungry Face at the same time will yield a chest with 1 Relic in it. These two relics effectively cancel each other's effects. If the player has not yet collected the Sapphire Key, then the relic will be linked to it as normal.

Anti-Synergies

  • It can be awkward to benefit from the extra relic from Matryoshka while also having Cursed Key since it will add a Curse to the player's deck (unless the player also has Omamori to prevent gaining the curse).

Trivia

  • The flavor text mentioning an unknown bird with white eyes and blue feathers references the Awakened One.
