Monsters are the normal Enemies of Slay the Spire. When these encounters are beaten, they drop a Card Reward, 10-20 Gold, and sometimes a Potion.

Ascension Modifiers

  • Ascension 2: Normal Enemies deal more damage.
  • Ascension 7: Normal Enemies have more health and will sometimes gain more Block.
  • Ascension 17: Normal Enemies have more challenging movesets and abilities.

Rules of Monster Encounters

  • The first 2 monster encounters (3 for Act 1) are randomly selected from an "Easy Pool", while the rest of the fights for that Act are randomly selected from a "Hard Pool".
    • In Act 1, you can never face these as your first "Hard Pool" combat depending on your 3rd "Easy Pool"
      • 3 Louses after 2 Louses
      • Swarm of Slimes or Large Slime after 2 Slimes
      • Exordium Thugs after Looter
      • Red Slaver or Exordium Thugs after Blue Slaver
    • In Act 3, you can never face 3 Darklings ("Hard Pool") after 3 Darklings ("Easy Pool"). These are identical fights.
  • The same monster encounter cannot be fought again in the next 2 monster encounters (that is, 3 consecutive monster encounters can never contain 2 identical fights).
