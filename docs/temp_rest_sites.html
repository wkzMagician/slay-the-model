Rest Sites

From Slay the Spire Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
See Map Locations for all possible rooms.

Rest Sites are a standard Map Location. The player must choose one of the available options to prepare for higher floors before proceeding. If one of the standard options is unavailable for some reason, it will be greyed out; if no other options are allowed, the Leave button will appear.

The last room before each boss in Acts 1, 2, and 3 is always a rest site.

Contents

  • 1 Standard Options
    • 1.1 Relic Modifiers
  • 2 Special Options
  • 3 Other Interactions

Standard Options

  • Rest: Heal for 30% of max health.
    • Greyed out by Coffee Dripper and Mark of the Bloom
    • Still available at full health.
  • Smith: Upgrade a card.
    • Greyed out by Fusion Hammer, or if all cards are already upgraded.
  • Recall: Obtain the Ruby Key.
    • Does not appear if Act 4 has not been revealed on the current save file, or after recalling at a previous rest site.

Relic Modifiers

  • Dream Catcher adds a card reward screen after Resting (affected by any card reward relics the player possesses).
  • Regal Pillow causes Resting to heal for an additional 15 HP.

Special Options

If one of the specified Relics has been picked up, more options are available at the Rest Site. Only two of these relics can be picked up in the same run.

  • Girya: Allows you to "Lift" to gain 1 Strength (up to three uses).
  • Peace Pipe: Allows you to "Toke" to remove a card from your deck.
  • Shovel: Allows you to "Dig" to acquire a random Relic.

Other Interactions

  • Eternal Feather restores 3 health for every five cards in your deck on entering the rest site automatically, without requiring an action.
  • After leaving the rest site, Ancient Tea Set will give two additional Energy on the first turn of the next combat.
