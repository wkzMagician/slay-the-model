Cursed Key

From Slay the Spire Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Cursed Key
Boss Relic
Any Class
Gain 1 Energy at the start of your turn. Whenever you open a non-Boss chest, obtain a Curse.
You can feel the malicious energy emanating from the key. Power comes at a price.
Energy
Resource used to play cards.

Cursed Key is a Boss Relic. It grants 1 Energy at the start of each turn in combat, at the cost of adding a Curse to the player's deck any time they open a non-Boss Chest. The Curse that gets added to the player's deck will never be one of Ascender's Bane, Curse of the Bell, Necronomicurse, or Pride.

Sources

  • Can be obtained from Boss Swap.
  • After fighting a Boss, Cursed Key can be offered as a choice of 3 Boss Relics.

Interactions

Order

  • The Curse is added to the player's deck immediately after opening the Chest.
    • If the Chest contains Omamori, it can never be used to block the Curse that was received from the chest that contained this relic.
    • If the player skips the Chest without opening it, they will avoid gaining a Curse.
    • If the player opens the Chest but does not collect any of its contents, they will still gain the Curse.

Synergies

  • As with all Energy relics, Cursed Key can allow the player to play high-impact expensive cards more easily.
  • Omamori, if already in the player's possession before opening the Chest, can be used to avoid gaining the Curse.
  • Darkstone Periapt will give the player +6 Max HP when a Curse is added to their deck.
  • Du-Vu Doll will provide the player with +1 Strength for each Curse in their deck.
  • Effects such as Blue Candle, Sever Soul, and Second Wind can allow the player to Exhaust Curses so that they won't be drawn again for the rest of that combat.
  • Relics such as Smiling Mask, The Courier, and Membership Card can make it cheaper to remove a Curse using the Merchant's card removal service.
  • Relics such as Peace Pipe and Empty Cage can be used to remove a Curse without visiting a Merchant.
  • Fire Breathing will trigger its damage when a Curse is drawn.

Anti-Synergies

  • Matryoshka can make it awkward to find Chests as the player will need to deal with gaining a Curse if they want the extra relic.
  • Tiny Chest will replace every 4th Unknown room with a Treasure Room, which can make it somewhat awkward to visit many of these rooms.
