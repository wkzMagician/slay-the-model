The Defect
75 (71 14) Health
 
Combat automaton which became self-aware. Ancient technology allows manipulation of Orbs.

The Defect is one of the 4 playable characters in the game. It starts with 75 (71 14) max HP.

Unlocks

The Defect is unlocked by completing (doesn't have to be a win) a run with the Silent.

Unlock Number Rewards
1 Rebound, Equilibrium, and Echo Form
2 TURBO, Sunder, and Meteor Strike
3 Hyperbeam, Recycle, and Core Surge
4 Gold-Plated Cables, Turnip, and Runic Capacitor
5 Data Disk, Symbiotic Virus, and Emotion Chip

Lore

When completing the Act 3 event Sensory Stone, you 'recall' what appear to be memories regarding the pasts of the characters. The [CONFUSION] dialogue option may recall the Defect right after they're implied to gain sentience, being approached by Neow.

CONFUSION

[OBJECTIVE] BALANCE must be ENFORCED
[DEFINE] BALANCE
[ERROR] BALANCE NOT FOUND
[DEFINE] BALANCE
[ERROR] BALANCE NOT FOUND
[WARNING] Large object aproaching

"I... ..am ....Neow.."

Trivia

  • With a character sprite width of 229 and height of 212.37, the Defect is the smallest character in the cast visually.
  • Is referred to as broken one by the Vampires in the Vampires event, as opposed to brother (Ironclad) or sister (Silent and Watcher)
